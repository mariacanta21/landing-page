The Problem

Most Interstate Investors Get Tasmania Wrong

Tasmania looks simple from the mainland. A handful of cities, affordable prices, strong rental demand. But without local knowledge, even experienced investors make the same costly mistakes.

01

Buying in the Wrong Suburb

Tasmania's capital growth is highly localised. The same street can outperform or underperform by 40%. Without a local lens, you are guessing — and guessing wrong is expensive.

02

Negotiating Alone

Selling agents work for the vendor, not you. Without independent representation, you sit across the table from a professional negotiator and typically pay $20,000 to $50,000 more than you should.

03

Missing the Off-Market Window

The best Tasmanian investment properties never reach the portals. They sell before they are listed, through relationships most interstate investors simply do not have.
