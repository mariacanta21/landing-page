The Model at a Glance

No Guesswork. A Repeatable System.

This is the exact process Timar runs to find, assess, cosmetically renovate and revalue investment properties in Tasmania.

Plan & Budget

Map out your time to buy, borrowing capacity and budget before the search begins.
